Airbnb Business Insights Dashboard | Power BI

This project simulates a real-world business scenario where a data analyst is required to analyze Airbnb market performance across major global cities. The objective is to help business teams understand market trends, pricing strategies, and customer experience factors that influence booking decisions.

The Power BI dashboard provides an analytical view of Airbnb listings by examining city-wise market share, listing growth trends, pricing differences across room types, and guest experience ratings. These insights can help platform managers, hosts, and business stakeholders identify high-performing markets, optimize pricing strategies, and improve customer trust.

Key Business Insights:
• Analysis of 279K+ Airbnb listings across 10 global cities  
• Identification of cities with the highest market share and listing concentration  
• Pricing comparison between Entire Place, Private Room, and Hotel Room listings  
• Evaluation of guest experience metrics such as cleanliness, communication, and value  
• Trust factors like verified identity and profile photo influencing booking confidence

Tools & Technologies:
Power BI | Data Analytics | Data Visualization | Business Intelligence
